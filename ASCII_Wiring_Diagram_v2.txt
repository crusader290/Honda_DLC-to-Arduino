Honda DLC 3-pin (Prelude '93):
    Pin2 = +12V
    Pin7 = K-Line
    Pin4 = GND

K-Line Protection & Nano RX Wiring:

 DLC Pin7 (K-Line)
       │
      [1kΩ]
       │
       +-------> Nano RX (Pin3)
       │
      ┌─────────┐
      │Schottky │
      │Diode    │
      │ Anode→RX│
      │ Cathode→DLC7
      └─────────┘
       │
      [47µF]
       │
      GND

Nano TX (Pin2) ───────> K-Line TX (through protection if desired, usually direct)

OLED:
 Nano A4 (SDA) --------> OLED SDA
 Nano A5 (SCL) --------> OLED SCL

Optional Notes:
- Schottky diode orientation is critical: **Anode to Nano RX**, **Cathode to DLC K-Line**.  
- 1kΩ resistor in series protects the Nano from current spikes.  
- 47µF capacitor across K-Line to GND smooths transients.  
- Buck converts 12V to stable 5V for Nano and OLED.  
- Fuse 0.5A on DLC Pin2 (+12V) for safety.
